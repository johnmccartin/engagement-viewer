About

Envision Cambridge spent its first year collecting feedback using our Mobile Engagement Station.

During the Listening Phase, we asked participants to mark their favorite and least favorite places, and where they would like to see change in the future. During the Visioning Phase, we asked participants to mark where they envision change for a better future.
