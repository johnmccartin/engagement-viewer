Envision Cambridge spent its first year collecting feedback using our Mobile Engagement Station, a giant table made to look like the map of Cambridge.

We asked participants to mark their favorite and least favorite places, and where they would like to see change in the future in green, red, and blue, respectively. We then photographed the marked up table and recorded that data using GIS (geographic information systems).
